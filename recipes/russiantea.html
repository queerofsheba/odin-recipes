Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter/margerine, soft
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 1/4 cup flour, sifted
  • 1/4tsp salt
  • 1tsp vanilla
  • 3/4 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Cream butter and vanilla until smooth
  2. Combine sugar and flour, then stir into butter mixture
  3. Mix in nuts
  4. Preheat oven to 400F
  5. Shape dough into 1" balls
  6. Bake 15 minutes
  7. While hot, roll baked balls in powdered sugar
  8. Once cooled, roll baked balls in powdered sugar again

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg, separated
  • 1/2tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/4tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup chopped nuts
  • jelly, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Mix butter, sugar, yolk, and vanilla
  3. Add flour and salt to mixture
  4. Shape dough into 1" balls
  5. Dip balls in beaten egg whites
  6. Roll balls in nuts
  7. Press thumb into middle of each ball, creating indentation
  8. Bake 10 minutes; once cooled, fill indentations with jelly

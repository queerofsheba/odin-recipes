Ingredients

  • 170g unsalted butter
  • 159g packed brown sugar
  • 255g molasses
  • 1tsp salt
  • 2tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4tsp allspice cloves
  • 1 large egg
  • 1tsp baking soda
  • 1/2tsp baking soda
  • 447g AP Flour

Steps

  1. Melt butter, then stir in sugar, molasses, salt, & spices
  2. Let mixture cool, then beat in egg
  3. Whisk together baking powder, baking soda & flour; add this to the molasses mixture
  4. Divide dough in half, shape into a thick rectangle; wrap and refrigerate for at least one hour
  5. Preheat oven to 350F; line baking sheets with parchment
  6. Flour clean work surface; roll out dough 1/8" to 1/4" thick; cut out shapes
  7. Transfer to baking sheet & bake 8-12 minutes or until firm; let cookies cool/set for several minutes before transferring to a cooling rack
